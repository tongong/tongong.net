about me

things i like

  • I like computers. They enable wonderful new ways of expression and open access to incredible amounts of knowledge. But I also dislike computers for being drivers of social isolation and the decline of democracy.
  • I like simplicity. There are rational justifications for this but I like it because it gives me a sense of comfort and beauty.
  • I like programming. Besides being an extremely convenient skill it enables you to "create the world" as Linus Torvalds put it aptly.
  • I like open-source. It captures all of the good things about computers.
  • I like Unix. I think I would prefer a microkernel OS (based on L4 for example) but I have not used such an OS yet.
  • I like JS/HTML. It's the most accessible and the most portable programming environment available today. I dislike npm and the attached ecosystem (no accessibility, no portability, no simplicity). I dislike CSS.
  • I like C. C feels like the original way things are supposed to be. Learning C makes you understand computers.
  • I like Lisp (Scheme). I have never used it though.
